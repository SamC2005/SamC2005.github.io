Why HyperText Markup Language is Annoying

The Repetitive Symbols

While the symbols make HTML easy to read, when writing the language, it takes time and effort. Spending effort on something isn't ever a bad thing, but it feels bad when after 10 minutes of writing you have a paragraph of "<" or ">" signs and about 3 lines of readable text.

The Confusing Data Tags

These tags are annoying to remember as they aren't real English. While after a bit of coding, you'll remember them easily, right now I dislike them a lot.
